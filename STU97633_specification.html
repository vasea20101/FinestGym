Objectives/Goals

 The primary mission of FinestGym is to facilitate the adoption of a more physically active and nutritionally sound way of life among its members. The second part of FinestGym's mission is to serve as a resource for our members as they navigate this transition.
  • improved flexibility of the muscles and density of the bones
  • better conditions of the brain and mind
  • supporting a healthy lifestyle and a balanced nutrition

Key audience

 The website caters to those who wish to enhance their general health and mental well-being, are seeking for the top gyms, or are already interested in working out at a gym. Anyone may utilise the gym at any time. There are no age- or gender-specific areas in this gym.

Provisional site structure

 The website will include 7 content web pages, including the following:

  • Home - the first page which is the website's "welcome" page
  • Membership - This page will show the membership guidance and policy. How to choose the right membership.
  • Coaches - a webpage that lists the trainers at the gym and their specialty areas
  • Blog - this web page will have several articles on plans in nutrition and training, many of which may be of great interest to our members.
  • FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions - the web page where users can find a list of commonly asked questions and answers regarding the website
  • About us - a webpage displaying the gym's goals and history
  • Contact - a webpage with the gym's contact details

Functional specification

 This website is built using HTML5 and CSS3, respectively. Users will be required to have a fundamental web browser in order to view the website.
